Producer-Producer

About

A simple job board for media producers.

Producer-Producer gathers public job postings from media, news, entertainment, audio, video, and social companies, then scores them so producer roles are easier to scan.

The board links back to the original employer posting for applications. Saved, applied, and digest features are account tools layered on top of the public feed.
