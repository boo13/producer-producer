Privacy Policy

Privacy for the job board.

Information We Collect

Producer-Producer stores the email address used for account access or digest signup. When signed in, the service may store saved jobs, applied jobs, passed jobs, digest preference, and related account settings.

How We Use It

We use account information to send sign-in codes, remember job actions, operate the daily digest when explicitly enabled, and keep the job board working reliably.

Service Providers

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages behind Cloudflare. The API runs on Fly.io. Email delivery may be handled by an email provider. These providers process data needed to operate the service.

Choices

Digest subscribers can unsubscribe from the email link or from account preferences. Account-related requests can be sent to hello@producer-producer.com.
